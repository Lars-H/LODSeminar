About us and what we do


Hi,

we are the Cepler-Team.
Ben, Nico, Lars.
Cepler stand for "Comparison Engine Processing Linked Endpoint Resources".
We want to exploit the linked data cloud in order to provide better comparisons.
People are bad a imagining large numbers.
Therefore, we provide a comparison, such that people can understand those numbers more intuitively.
