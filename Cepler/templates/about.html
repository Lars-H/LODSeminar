What Cepler is simply explained.


Cepler - A comparison engine

Cepler is a project that is part of a seminar of the Institute of Applied Informatics and Formal Description Methods (AIFB) at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT).
The purpose of this seminar is to develop a Web 3.0 application that exploits the Linked Data Cloud in order to solve a problem.
The problem that we want to tackle is the fact that people are very bad at imagining large numbers. Therefore, we semantically use the data of linked data endpoints to provide a good comparison.
