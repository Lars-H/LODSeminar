What Cepler is simply explained.


Cepler - A comparison engine

Cepler is a project we launched in the course of a seminar at the Institute of Applied Informatics and Formal Description Methods (AIFB) at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT).
The purpose of this seminar is to develop a Web 3.0 application that exploits the Linked Open Data Cloud in order to solve a problem.
We decided to tackle the fact that people are very bad at imagining large numbers. Therefore, we semantically use the data of linked data endpoints to provide a good comparison. Cepler stands for "Comparison Engine Processing Linked Endpoint Resources" - providing a comparison, so people can understand those numbers more intuitively.
